What is DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO?

This is a mimic description text explaining that DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO is a local initiative in Kibungo Sector to set, monitor and celebrate family-level performance contracts aligned with sector and national priorities.

Why a digital platform?

The web application simplifies data collection, ensures transparency, and enables every family to participate actively, either directly or with the help of Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga or local authorities.

Scalable for the future

The system is designed to scale beyond Kibungo Sector to cover other sectors and districts, and eventually the entire country, while keeping the same simple and friendly user experience.

Key Features of the System

  • ✔ Family registration with full member profiles.
  • ✔ Individual and shared performance duties (imihigo).
  • ✔ Progress tracking and simple status dashboards.
  • ✔ Support for Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga and authorities to assist families.
