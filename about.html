Context and Rationale

This is placeholder text that explains that Kibungo Sector is committed to improving the welfare and socio-economic status of its residents through accountable, measurable and inclusive development programs. The DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO initiative brings performance contracts down to the family level so that every household participates actively in the sector's progress.

Another paragraph of mimic content can highlight that families co-create their goals in areas such as education, health, agriculture, income-generating activities, hygiene, and social cohesion. These goals are then captured in the digital platform and monitored over time.

Objectives

  • Promote a culture of planning and accountability within households.
  • Align family goals with sector and national development priorities.
  • Strengthen collaboration between families, community leaders and local government.
  • Provide reliable data to guide decision making at all levels.

Target Beneficiaries

Mimic text indicating that the program targets all families living in Kibungo Sector, with special attention to vulnerable households, youth-headed families and families with persons with disabilities. Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga and local leaders help those who have limited access to technology.

How Performance Contracts Work

  1. Family members discuss and agree on key goals for the year.
  2. They categorize goals into individual duties and shared duties.
  3. Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga or an authority assists with data entry if needed.
  4. Progress is updated regularly through the portal.
  5. Achievements are recognized and challenges addressed in collaboration with leaders.
